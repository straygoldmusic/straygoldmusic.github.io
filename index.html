ABOUT

“This time, it’s personal.” - ‘90s action movie quote and mantra for Stray Gold. Singer-songwriter Micah Smith has spent most of his music career with indie-rock act Empty Atlas. But as Stray Gold, his first solo outing, he wanted to take a different approach, and not just by leaning into his pop and Americana sensibilities.

“These songs are kind of unfiltered stories about myself,” Smith says, “including the things that may not be so flattering.”

That’s an element you’ll hear right off the bat in Stray Gold’s debut single, “Stupid Answers,” out everywhere February 23. Smith wrote the song about his habit of filling awkward moments with jokes that don’t always land.

“It’s me sort of telling on myself, but also, I think it’s a universal fact of growing up and figuring out who you are,” he says. “You’re going to say the wrong things and feel awkward and learn from it.”
